# Student Guide (Phase 1)

## Getting started
Open the site (Hindi by default; switch to English anytime with the language toggle — your choice is
remembered). Tap **Login** and enter your mobile number. We send a 6-digit OTP; enter it to sign in.
You can also continue with Google. You never pick a role — every student account is a Student account.

## Your dashboard
After signing in you land on your dashboard, designed to answer **"What should I study today?"**
rather than showing a wall of videos. In Phase 4 this fills with your daily plan, continue-watching,
daily quiz, current affairs, revision, and progress. Phase 1 confirms your account, language
preference, and secure session.

## Language
Hindi and English are both first-class. Questions and key terms can appear bilingually. The site is
never machine-translated — content is professionally managed in both languages.

## Privacy & security
Your session is protected with secure, http-only cookies. Course access (added in Phase 6) is granted
through verified purchases (entitlements), never a simple on/off flag.
