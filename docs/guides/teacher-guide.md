# Teacher / Reviewer / Question Setter Guide (Phase 1)

You receive an **invitation email** from an administrator. Open the link, set a password, and your
account activates with a role and course/subject scope already assigned — you never choose your own
role.

Sign in at `/admin/login`. You land on your workspace:
- **Teacher → My Content**, **Reviewer → Review Queue**, **Question Setter → Question Bank**.

In Phase 1 these workspaces are shells that confirm your identity, role, and scope. The full authoring,
review, and publishing tools arrive in Phases 3 and 5. Key rules that already apply:
- You can only work within your assigned courses/subjects.
- Teachers and Question Setters submit content for review; **they cannot publish**.
- Reviewers approve or request corrections; **they cannot publish**. Only Content Admins publish.
Every action is checked on the server and audited.
